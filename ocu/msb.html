MSB Commencement and Luncheon - May 9, 2015

Saturday, May 9, 2015

Henry J. Freede Wellness and Activity Center
NW 27th Street and Florida Ave.

Timeline of Events

Baccalaureate | 9:00 a.m. Bishop W. Angie Smith Chapel Undergraduate Commencement | 11:00 a.m. MSB Celebration Luncheon | 1:00 - 2:30 p.m. Register Graduate Commencement | 3:00 p.m.

The May 2015 graduate and undergraduate ceremonies will be the only commencements held for the 2014-15 academic year. December 2014, May 2015, and August 2015 graduates are all encouraged to participate on this date. If your degree requirements will not be complete by the summer conferral date of July 31, but you would like to participate in the May 9 ceremony, please contact Academic Affairs (405) 208-5287.

The deadline date to apply for your diploma and graduation is February 27, 2015.

Special Needs Seating for those who are unable to climb stairs is available on a first-come, first-served basis. The Freede Wellness Center is wheelchair accessible. A sign language interpreter is on stage at the ceremony. If you have guests with other special needs, please contact Academic Affairs at (405) 208-5287.

Tickets are not required for the ceremonies, however, seating is available on a first-come, first-served basis. The Henry J. Freede Wellness and Activity Center will open at 9:45 a.m. for the undergraduate ceremony and at 1:45 p.m. for the graduate ceremony. All guests must be in their seats no later than fifteen minutes before the ceremony is scheduled to begin.
